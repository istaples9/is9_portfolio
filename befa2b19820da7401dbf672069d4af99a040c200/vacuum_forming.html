About Project
Engineered a thermoformed seat for high volume production. Solid surface plastic is vacuum formed into the contour of a seat using a mold. The seat form is then trimmed with a fixture that guides a handheld router, cutting the edge of the form to a fixed depth. After trimming the form is glued to a particle board sub-frame and finish sanded.
