is9 - Engagement Ring

Design and 3D modeling of an engagement ring & wedding ring set for my spouse. The gem on the engagement ring is uniquely set via a groove on the inside sweep of the ring. The ring was modeled and rendered using Blender, then 3D printed out of silver.
